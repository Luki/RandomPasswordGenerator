Overview

This Random Password Generator is designed with user privacy and security as a top priority.
The tool operates entirely locally and does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or generated passwords.

No Storage of Passwords

Passwords generated by this tool are not saved, logged, or stored in any way.
Once generated, they exist only temporarily on your device and are never retained by the application.

No Data Transmission

Generated passwords and user activity are not sent to any servers, databases, third parties, or external systems.
The generator does not communicate with any external services.

No Third-Party Access

No data is shared with third parties, including companies, organizations, or individuals.
Your usage remains completely private.

Security

Because all operations are performed locally on your device and no data leaves your system,
this tool is safe to use for anyone requiring secure password generation.
